## Overview
Complete project management plan for launching a community-centered coffee shop in Kansas City, Missouri by December 15, 2025. This project demonstrates end-to-end project management skills including scope definition, stakeholder analysis, risk management, budgeting, and scheduling.

**Project Objective:** Launch a welcoming, 25-seat coffee shop offering high-quality drinks and pastries within a $25,000 budget and 3-month timeline.

## Stakeholder Analysis

![Stakeholder Register](stakeholder_register.png)

**Key Stakeholders Managed:**
- **Shop Owner** (High Power/Interest) - Weekly email updates, biweekly in-person meetings
- **Project Manager** (Me) - Continuous oversight, weekly status reports
- **Suppliers** - Monthly performance reviews, email for orders
- **Interior Designer** - Design review meetings, email communication
- **Marketing Consultant** - Weekly coordination during Nov-Dec launch phase
- **Baristas & Cashier** - Text and in-person training before Nov 20
- **Health Inspector** - Formal communication pre-opening
- **Customers** - Social media engagement (Nov-Dec)
